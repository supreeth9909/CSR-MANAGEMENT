📋 Food Pre-Order Instructions & Penalty Rules
How to Order
  1. Select your break time slot
  2. Add items from one stall only (multi-stall orders not allowed)
  3. Place your order before the break slot starts
  4. Collect your food during the selected break time
  5. Payment is made at the stall during pickup (Cash/UPI)
⚠️ Penalty for Missed Orders

If you order food but don't pick it up, the stall wastes prepared food. Penalties apply:
